Module 4: Applying Safe Work Practices

← Back to Home Page ← Back to Task 3 Modules

4.1 Before Starting Work

  • Inspect Your Tools/Equipment: Check that tools, machinery, and equipment are in good working order.
  • Check the Work Area: Remove unnecessary debris and mark off-limits zones.
  • Set Up Barriers or Signs: Use warning signs or barriers if needed to protect others.
  • Use Correct Lifting Technique: Bend knees, keep load close, or use mechanical aids.
  • Warm-Up: Stretch or warm up muscles before heavy or repetitive work.

4.2 During the Work

  • Follow the Sequence: Stick to planned steps, no shortcuts.
  • Use PPE Properly: Wear and maintain required personal protective equipment.
  • Stay Focused: Avoid distractions and inattention.
  • Monitor Conditions: Re-assess if environment or tasks change.
  • Communicate: Keep team aware of any issues.
  • Control Debris: Clean up scraps and spills promptly.
  • Maintain Separation: Keep others clear of hazardous zones.

4.3 After Finishing Work

  • Turn Off and Secure Equipment: Power down, unplug, and store tools.
  • Clean Up Debris: Sweep dust and scrap to prevent hazards.
  • Inspect Your Work: Check for leftover hazards or stability issues.
  • Report Issues: Log any unsafe conditions or near misses.

4.4 Staying Alert

  • Look Out for Others: Encourage safe practices among coworkers.
  • Adapt: Pause work and re-plan if new hazards appear.
