Overview of Requirements

To be deemed competent, you must successfully complete three assessment sections, working in a team to plan and execute a basic construction project while applying sustainable practices.

  • Section 1: Written Questioning. This tests your knowledge of the construction industry, job roles, teamwork, environmental requirements, and basic construction principles.
  • Section 2: Product-Based Methods. In your team, you will produce a portfolio of project documents, including a Material List, Construction Procedure, JSA, a personal Skills Evaluation, and an Environmental Incident Report.
  • Section 3: Direct Observation. An assessor will observe your team planning and building the project. You must demonstrate teamwork, safe work practices, accurate construction (to a �2mm tolerance), and proper cleanup procedures.

You must achieve a "Satisfactory" result in all parts of all three sections to be judged competent.
